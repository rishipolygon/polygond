About Polygond

A small, slow finance journal.

Markets · Energy · Capital

Polygond is a small, slow finance journal. No tickers flashing, no hot takes by the hour — just considered writing about markets, energy, and how capital moves through the world.

The aim is simple: fewer, better notes. Things worth reading twice. Each piece tries to earn its place rather than fill a schedule.
