About the firm

A focused investor in energy & capital

Polygond is a research-led investment firm. We commit capital across the energy value chain and the markets that price it — from upstream operators to the infrastructure and trading desks that move barrels and electrons.

We are deliberately small, deliberately slow, and deliberately concentrated. Fewer positions, held longer, understood better.
