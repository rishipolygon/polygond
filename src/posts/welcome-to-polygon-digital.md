Polygon Digital is a weekly research note on the places where **physical markets meet computation** — energy term structure, basis and differentials, storage economics, and the growing role of machine intelligence in how risk gets priced.

## What to expect

One note per week, usually structured around a single question:

- Why is a curve shaped the way it is?
- What is a spread actually compensating you for?
- Where does a model help, and where does it just add confidence to a guess?

No paywall, no sponsorships, no positions talked. Just the work.

## How it's built

This site is deliberately simple: plain Markdown files rendered by a small React app, two colours, three typefaces. The constraint is the point — if a chart or a table can't carry the argument in black and white, the argument probably isn't ready.

> Write to think. Publish to find out if you were right.

See you next week.
